About the author

Born in Marseille, I like to say that I am a Mediterranean kid.

After achieving the preparatory classes, I left the south to join Ecole-Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay where I have studied civil engineering during two years. I learned about a lot of different topics, ranging from fluid and solid mechanics to signal analysis. Then, I decided to pursue a master degree in Energy at Mines ParisTech. At that time, I was eager to know about renewables and the energy market. After all these years learning about applied physics, I chose to follow the Master MVA (Mathematics, Vision and Learning) to deeper my knowledge on optimization and machine learning.

Beside research, I'm fond of skiing and mountain biking.
