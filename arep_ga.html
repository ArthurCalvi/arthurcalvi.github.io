Genetic Algorithm : second application in civil engineering & architecture

During my second internship, I worked at l'Hypercube°AREP on the optimization of the GHG emissions, comfort and cost of buildings. AREP is a subsidiary of SNCF specialized in the renovation and conception of train stations. During 6 months, I have implemented a multiploïd non-sorted genetic algorithm to handle the multi-objective optimization. Multiploidy (having several copies for each chromosome) allows a certain flexibility to handle the compatibility issues between materials and wall systems.
