Deep learning : texture generation

Texture synthesis has undergone spectacular developments in recent years by generating images whose responses to certain neural networks are constrained. A strong limitation of these approaches is the huge number of parameters implemented for each example texture. Recently an alternative approach has been proposed with the Neural Cellular Automata. This approach is based on a very simple neural architecture which approaches both cellular automata and reaction-diffusion equations. Less universal than other methods, this method nevertheless allows to generate a large variety of textures.
